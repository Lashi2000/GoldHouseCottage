About

This beautiful lakeside villa in Port Colborne, located within 30 minutes from Niagara Falls, Ontario, is the perfect retreat for families, couples, or small groups to relax and enjoy your own private beach with panoramic views of Lake Erie and stunning sunsets. The 5-bedroom, 3.5-bathroom villa features a spacious layout, cozy furnishings, and a full kitchen—excellent for fishing and swimming in very clean water.
